Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?
Each project is unique, so prices vary. I always provide a detailed and transparent quote before starting. The first exchange is free and without commitment.
How long does it take?
It depends on the project complexity. For a simple map: 1-2 weeks. For a more complex project: 1-2 months. I always give you a realistic schedule from the start.
Do I need technical training to use your tools?
No. My tools are designed to be used by non-experts. If necessary, I train you on how to use them, but the goal is for you to be autonomous quickly.
What types of data do you work with?
Excel, CSV, databases, open data... I adapt to your formats. If your data is in a particular format, we discuss it.
Can you host the tools you create?
Yes, I can host your tools. We discuss together the best solution according to your needs and budget.
