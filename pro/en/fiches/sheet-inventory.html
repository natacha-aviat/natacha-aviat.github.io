Inventory tool

Food and hygiene product scanner for MaMaMa association

The association conducts several collections per year in supermarkets. These collections are organized by teams of volunteers who collect specific food products for babies (formula, baby food...) and hygiene products for babies (diapers, wipes, etc.) and their mothers (pads, toothpaste, etc.). Volunteers must then establish an inventory of collected products and weigh them for reporting purposes.

❌ Before

  • Manual counting and weighing
  • Time-consuming information search

✅ After

  • Instant barcode scan with own smartphone
  • Immediate nutritional data
  • Easy export to spreadsheet
  • Mobile tool used in the field
