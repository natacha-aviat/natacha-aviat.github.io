MedLex (Au Clair)

Web application that guides freelance nurses through their contractual needs — replacement, collaboration, shared practice arrangements. Legal concepts are explained step by step, without unnecessary jargon, with templates compliant with French public health and nursing ethics codes, validated by Me Violaine, a health law attorney.

❌ Before

  • Fear of drafting poorly or signing too quickly
  • Clauses not understood, anxiety around paperwork
  • Generic templates not suited to nursing practice

✅ After

  • Guided questionnaire, one question at a time
  • Instant preview with each clause explained
  • Clean PDF ready to send and sign with confidence
  • Review and validation by a specialized attorney

User journey

1. Guided questionnaire:

The nurse answers simple questions about her situation (continuous or discontinuous replacement, billing, duration…). The tool translates answers into adapted legal clauses.

2. Preview and export:

Each important passage is highlighted and explained. The user can edit, go back, then download a PDF ready to sign.
