Industrial water discharges in France

Interactive mapping of water discharges from industrial facilities in France, allowing to identify and visualize discharge volumes by type (connected to the sewer network or isolated in the environment).

The map distinguishes two types of discharges:

  • Connected discharges: water discharged to the collective sewer network
  • Isolated discharges: water discharged directly into the environment (rivers, aquifers, etc.)

Interactive map using Leaflet to display industrial facilities with their discharge volumes. Each facility is represented by a pie chart marker indicating the proportion of each discharge type (connected/isolated). The pie chart size indicates the total discharge flow amplitude. Detailed information is available on click (facility name, address, discharge volumes in m³ per year by type).
