Industrial water withdrawals in France

Interactive mapping of water withdrawals from industrial facilities in France, allowing to identify and visualize withdrawal volumes by water source.

The map distinguishes three types of withdrawals:

  • Groundwater: withdrawals from aquifers
  • Surface water: withdrawals from rivers, lakes, etc.
  • Distribution network: withdrawals from the drinking water network

Interactive map using Leaflet to display industrial facilities with their withdrawal volumes. Each facility is represented by a pie chart marker indicating the proportion of each withdrawal type (groundwater, surface water, distribution network). The pie chart size indicates the total withdrawal flow amplitude. Detailed information is available on click (facility name, address, withdrawal volumes in m³ by source).
