Emily in Paris

Interactive map of museums and beauty spots in Paris, custom-built for Emily Lutzker, a New Yorker visiting the city. A personalized selection of 20+ addresses — from the Louvre to Citypharma — with honest notes to help prioritize without getting lost in endless travel guides.

❌ Before

  • Scattered lists across Instagram, Google Maps and blogs
  • No geographic overview
  • Hard to know what to visit based on taste and energy level

✅ After

  • Interactive map with all locations geolocated
  • Art & Beauty filters and thematic tags (Impressionism, Luxury, Iconic…)
  • Personal curated notes for each address
  • Sort by favorites or alphabetical order, built-in search

Methodology

1. Personalized curation:

Based on a conversation with Emily about her interests (art, beauty, visit pace), I selected and annotated each address: museums (Louvre, Orsay, Pinault Collection…), beauty department stores (Le Bon Marché, Printemps Haussmann) and iconic spots (Citypharma, Sephora Champs-Élysées).

2. Interactive map:

Using Leaflet and JavaScript, I built a lightweight, elegant map designed for a mobile user on the go.

  • Color-coded markers by category (art, beauty or mixed).
  • Side panel with filterable list, search and detailed notes on click.
  • Thematic tags to refine the selection based on the day's mood.
